Hostel Rules

Hostel accomodation is availabl for the outstation students. On arrival, astudents will report to the Hostel Superintendent of their hostel and will take permission of the room allotted to him/her after signing the inventory of the furniture, electrical items in the rooms.

The students of a room are responsible for any damage to the furniture, fitting, walls etc. of the room allotted to them.

No resident is allowed to change his/her room without permission of the respective Hostel Superintendent.

Any act of physical violence, wilful damage to property or drunken and notorious behaviour constitutes an offence which will be brought before the DISCIPILINARY COMMITTEE for action.

Use of narcotics, consumption of alcoholic beverages and gambling in any form are strictly prohibited. If any student in the hostel campus found to be invloved in any of these activities, he/she will be punished and will be compelled to pay a fine of Rs. 5000/-. The matter shall be brought to the notice of the DISCIPILINARY COMMITTEE and the GUARDIAN of the student.

Use of audio systems within the hostel premises is strictly prohibited without prior permission from the Hostel Superintendent.

Use of heater, cookers etc. is strictly forbidden in the hostel. Misuse of electrical installations and wiring is punishable offence. Such use will attract penalty by way of fine.

No parties, other entertainment programs, meeting would be organized in the hostel campus without prior permission of Hostel Superintendent

Our Facilities

MCET provides numourous facilities to the hostelers including

Medical Assistance

Hostelers get medical assitance as and when needed from the medical cell provided by the college. In case of any emergency students are taken to the nearest hospital, Murshidabad Medical College and hospital.

Sports and Games

The hostel is equipped with a medium sized playground where hostelers can play various games. Cricket is an all time game to the hostelers and eventually Volleyball and Badminton becomes popular in winter.

Canteen

The hostel has canteen facilities. Hostel boarders as well as day scholars can have their meals at the canteen.The hostelers can have their lunch and dinner with a monthly meal system or can also have with daily meal system.

Free 24X7 Wifi

Murshidabad College of Engineering and Technology provides wifi facilities to its hostel students at free of cost. The hostel has 4 active jio-fiber connections and a router in every building patched with the connections.
